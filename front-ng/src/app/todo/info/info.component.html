Attention! When guitar in focus all browser's keyboard's events (hotkeys) are disabled and you can't use it. For example, if you press Ctrl+A all text will not be selected, or if you press Ctrl+S web page will not be saved. That's why please use complex chords careful or disabled some hotkeys in your operation system.
