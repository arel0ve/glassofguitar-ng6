You can play the guitar with your mouse and keyboard. Guitar panel must be in focus (blue border around it) for using keyboard. If you click on any object except guitar, you lose focus and can't use keyboard anymore. For return to guitar panel you have to click on it or press Tab.

Fingerboard
To draw chords on fingerboard you can use your keyboard next way:
  • Major simple chords - X: press and stay down A, B, C, D, E, F, G
  • Upper chords - X#: press and stay down Shift+... keys of simple chords.
  • Minor chords - Xm: press and stay down Ctrl+... keys of simple chords.
  • Dominant 7 chords - X7: press and stay down Alt+... keys of simple chords.
To create complex chord you can use all modified keys, such as you have to key down Ctrl+Shift+Alt+A for creating A#m7 chord.
To make capo on fingerboard you have to press and release keys from ~ for capo 0 and 1, 2 ... + for capo 1, 2 ... 12. Second press on one key take off capo from fingerboard.

Press Delete or Backspace to clear fingerboard and release all strings.

For change volume you have to turn up or down ðŸ–° wheel on free area above fingerboard. Min volume = 0%, max volume = 100%.
For change speed of playing all strings you have to turn up or down ðŸ–° wheel on free area below fingerboard. Min speed means that it's delay 210 ms before playing next string, when playing all of it (when you press â†‘ or â†“). Max speed means that this delay is 10 ms. And 1% of speed changing is 2 ms delay.

Attention! When guitar in focus all browser's keyboard's events (hotkeys) are disabled and you can't use it. For example, if you press Ctrl+A all text will not be selected, or if you press Ctrl+S web page will not be saved. But all keyboard's events (hotkeys) of your operation system stay enabled anyway, such as Ctrl+Shift+Esc open Task Manager or Ctrl+F4 close the active window in Windows operation system. That's why please use complex chords careful or disabled some hotkeys in your operation system.
